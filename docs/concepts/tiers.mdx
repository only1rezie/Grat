---
title: "The Three Tiers"
description: "Prism is organized into three levels of diagnostic depth."
---

Prism builds on itself. Most sessions end at Tier 1. The rare hard cases escalate to Tier 2. Tier 3 is for instruction-level investigation.

## Tier 1 — Decode
**The Fast Path.**
Prism fetches the transaction, classifies the error, looks it up in the taxonomy, and resolves contract codes. This runs in under two seconds.

## Tier 2 — Trace
**The Diagnostic Path.**
Prism reconstructs the exact ledger state at the time of execution and replays the transaction in a modified Soroban sandbox. It produces a hierarchical execution timeline and resource profile.

## Tier 3 — Time-Travel
**The Investigation Path.**
Adds interactive debugging to the replay: breakpoints, step-through, and **What-If mode**. Re-simulate with modified inputs (arguments, state, limits) and see the diff in outcomes without redeploying.
